WHAT DOES YOUR NAME MEAN? - Reaksmey Sor

My name is defined as power in English word. It doesn’t mean anything in Khmer. It might be, but probably I don’t realize. In my country (Cambodia), it was named for both genders. So many Khmer celebrities are having this name, footballer, boxer, etc. The reason behind this name is like the bag of M&M. Little did everyone knows, it is also a combination name of my parents. My father’s name is Dyne (Dee-Neh) and my mother's name is Vanna (Van-Nah). Dyna (Dee-Na). The special reason that they named me this was to keep their name to be part of my soul and spirit forever. I definitely love this. My parents are energetic, work hard and proactive. My father is a problem-quick solver and my mom is a sweet woman who always encourages and cheers me up. I start to see this world on December 18th, 2004. I inherit half of their names and I want to inherit their personality as well. “Dyna” keeps me to remember my special people who let me see this world forever. I am proud to have this name. I also haven’t seen someone who names their children the combination of their name yet. To be honest, it is the most meaningful name that I’ve ever asked for.
